Our Mission.

The problem we see in the current scenario is that an individual looking to get started in the space of tech has to face a lot of problems, such as where to start, what to learn, how to get better, and where to get the right opportunities for their skill set. Our goal is to empower and help individuals to grow and achieve their purpose in life whether it be getting started in computer science or be it learning tech stack to get ready for a great career, We are a non-profit community that aims to solve these issues and provide resources for people.
